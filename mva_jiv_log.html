How MVA-JIV LOG Works
For Field Staff
Every time you use AI to help a veteran — writing case notes, researching benefits, preparing for court, drafting referrals — log it here. The system calculates tokens saved, time saved, and dollar value returned to veteran services.
Interaction Types
Case Note
AI-assisted documentation of veteran case activity
Benefits
VA benefits eligibility research and claims guidance
Court Prep
Preparing documentation for VTC hearings
Referral
LACADA, VPAN, housing, employment referral letters
VA Claims
526EZ, 21-22, 20-0995 claim processing assistance
Progress Report
Court progress and Board of Supervisors submissions
What the Savings Mean
🟢 Tokens — fewer words = less computing power
⚡ Energy — electricity saved in data centers
💧 Water — cooling water saved at data centers
🌿 CO₂ — carbon reduction from energy savings
💰 Dollar value — staff time + compute cost saved
Shared Database
All entries save to a shared server — not just your device. Every VSO sees the same data. Cameron Cook's supervisor view shows all staff activity for Board of Supervisors and JCOD grant reports.
Admin — Cameron Cook Only
Supervisor login unlocks the Admin Panel — add or remove VSOs, reset individual or all logs, and export official reports.
