How This Works
Purpose
Offline Mode
Toggle Online/Offline in the header, or it activates automatically when connectivity is lost. Entries save locally on your device. When WiFi returns, all entries sync automatically. Built after the Altadena and Malibu fires — when systems failed and veterans still needed help.
Wellness Seconds
Every second IGM governance saves from verbose AI output is a second returned to direct veteran care. A social work metric for a social work system — not dollars.
