About the Work

The Inference Governance Module (IGM) is the first infrastructure-layer AI governance system designed for public sector deployment. Built on the Transformational Accountability Ecosystem (TAE) framework developed over 15 years in California correctional and public service settings, the IGM applies the same accountability principles to artificial intelligence.

Every public agency AI inference — governed or ungoverned — is automatically classified, logged, and reported. The human regulator never has to trust anyone. The system audits itself.
